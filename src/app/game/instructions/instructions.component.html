How to Play Minesweeper

Your goal is to reveal all the safe cells in the grid without clicking on a mine. The numbers on revealed cells tell you how many mines are adjacent. Use flags to mark cells where you suspect mines are hidden. The game ends when all safe cells are revealed or you click on a mine.

Adjust Difficulty

Customize the difficulty by choosing different grid sizes and adjusting the number of mines. Tap the "Setup" button to fine-tune your game settings for a personalized challenge.
